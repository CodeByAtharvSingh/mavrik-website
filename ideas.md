# Mavrik Website — Design Ideas

## Three Approaches

### Approach 1: "Forge" — Industrial Precision
Bold black and orange, heavy typography, sharp edges. Feels like a weapon.
**Probability: 0.04**

### Approach 2: "Signal" — Clean Tech Manifesto  
White-dominant, orange as a signal color, editorial layout. Feels like a premium software brand.
**Probability: 0.07**

### Approach 3: "Ember" — Warm Intelligence
Off-white base with deep charcoal text, orange as fire/energy. Asymmetric layout, bold display type. Feels alive and trustworthy.
**Probability: 0.03**

---

## ✅ CHOSEN: Approach 2 — "Signal"

### Design Movement
Editorial Tech — inspired by premium SaaS brands like Linear, Vercel, and Arc. Clean, confident, and purposeful.

### Core Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — every element earns its place
2. **Orange as signal** — used sparingly for maximum impact (CTAs, highlights, accents)
3. **Bold typographic hierarchy** — headlines do the heavy lifting
4. **Light with depth** — white base with subtle shadows, not flat

### Color Philosophy
- Background: `#F8F7F4` (warm white, not clinical)
- Foreground: `#1A1A1A` (near-black, warm)
- Primary: `#E85D04` (Mavrik Orange — ownable, energetic)
- Secondary: `#FF8C42` (lighter orange for gradients)
- Muted: `#6B6B6B` (secondary text)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (cards, panels)

### Layout Paradigm
Asymmetric editorial grid. Hero section: left-aligned headline, right side has floating abstract 3D orb/shape. Sections alternate between full-width and constrained content. Stats section breaks the grid with oversized numbers.

### Signature Elements
1. **Orange gradient orb** — abstract glowing sphere in hero, echoes throughout
2. **Oversized display numbers** — stats rendered at 80-100px, commanding attention
3. **Diagonal section breaks** — subtle angled dividers between sections

### Interaction Philosophy
Smooth, purposeful. Hover states reveal depth. Scroll triggers entrance animations. Nothing decorative — every animation confirms an action or reveals content.

### Animation
- Entrance: fade-up with 40px translate, 0.4s ease-out, staggered 80ms
- Hover on cards: subtle lift (translateY -4px), shadow deepens
- CTA button: scale(0.97) on press, 160ms
- Stats: count-up animation on scroll into view

### Typography System
- Display: `Syne` (bold, geometric, distinctive) — headlines
- Body: `Inter` (clean, readable) — paragraphs, UI
- Mono: `JetBrains Mono` — code snippets, version numbers
- Hierarchy: 72px hero → 48px section → 32px sub → 18px body → 14px caption

### Brand Essence
**Mavrik: Private AI for people who don't compromise.** Precise. Sovereign. Fast.

### Brand Voice
Headlines: Direct, declarative. "Your AI. Your Machine. No Cloud."
CTAs: Action-first. "Download Free" not "Get Started Today"
Banned: "Welcome to", "Get started today", "Revolutionary", "Cutting-edge"

### Wordmark & Logo
A bold "M" mark — angular, like a signal wave or mountain peak. Orange on white. Monospace wordmark "MAVRIK" in caps beside it.

### Signature Brand Color
`#E85D04` — Mavrik Orange. Warm, energetic, unmistakable.
